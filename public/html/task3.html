Demographic


Name: Tran Thi Thuy
Occupation: University Student
Majoring: Marketing Management
Year: 1st year

Goal


• Be successful in her academic career: She is determined to excel in her studies and achieve academic success. She aims to gain knowledge, develop new skills, and perform well in her university courses.
• Make the most of the Learning Management System (LMS): She wants to fully utilize the university's LMS to access course materials, engage with her peers and instructors, and enhance her learning experience.

Pain Points


• Confusing navigation: She has trouble navigating the LMS interface. She prefers a user-friendly design that allows her to easily locate and access the desired content and features.
• Overwhelming wall of text: She prefers visual aids and concise information over excessive text. She finds it challenging to process large amounts of text and prefers visually engaging content.
• Useless features within modules: She feels frustrated when encountering unnecessary or redundant features within course modules. She desires a streamlined experience where every feature serves a clear purpose and enhances her learning.
• Lack of details/preview in hypertext content: She suggests that hypertext content should provide more details or previews to help her decide which content to explore. This would make navigation more efficient and allow her to make informed choices.
